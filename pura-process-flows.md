## 1. Aloe & Green Tea Hand Sanitizer

**Category:** Hand Sanitizer
**Tagline:** Kills 99.9% germs. Soothes every time.
**Color Theme:** Greens — #d4ecd5 → #4a6b4e

### Key Ingredients
| Ingredient | Source | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Aloe Vera Gel | Organic farms, Rajasthan, India | Humectant, anti-inflammatory, skin repair |
| Green Tea Extract | Darjeeling cold-extraction | Antioxidant (EGCG), anti-bacterial boost |
| Ethanol 70% | Pharmaceutical-grade, sugarcane-derived | Primary antimicrobial agent |
| Glycerin | Plant-derived (palm-free) | Moisture retention, skin softening |
| Carbomer | Cosmetic grade | Gel thickener, texture stabiliser |
| Aloe Vera Fragrance | Natural terpene blend | Scent profile |

### Process Flow (6 Steps)

**Step 1 — Ethical Harvest**
- Aloe vera leaves hand-harvested at 3-year maturity (highest gel concentration)
- Green tea leaves plucked at first flush (highest catechin content)
- Sourcing certified by ECOCERT organic standard
- Batch traceability code assigned at farm level
- *Quality gate: moisture content, pesticide residue test*

**Step 2 — Cold Extraction**
- Aloe leaves filleted within 4 hours of harvest to prevent oxidation
- Inner gel cold-pressed at <10°C to preserve acemannan polysaccharides
- Green tea cold-extracted in purified water at 40°C for 45 minutes
- Extract filtered through 0.2 micron membrane
- *Quality gate: HPLC test for EGCG concentration (min 15mg/g)*

**Step 3 — Ethanol Preparation**
- Pharmaceutical-grade ethanol (96%) diluted to 70% v/v with purified water
- pH adjusted to 6.2–6.8 (skin-safe range)
- Ethanol tested for methanol contamination (must be <0.1%)
- *Quality gate: Gas chromatography purity test*

**Step 4 — Formulation & Blending**
- Carbomer dispersed in purified water, neutralised with triethanolamine
- Aloe gel + green tea extract blended into carbomer base
- Glycerin added at 2% concentration for moisture lock
- Ethanol 70% incorporated under controlled mixing (prevents gel breakdown)
- Fragrance added at 0.3% — below sensitisation threshold
- *Quality gate: Viscosity test (4000–6000 cP), pH 6.2–6.8*

**Step 5 — Stability & Safety Testing**
- 28-day accelerated stability test at 40°C/75% humidity
- Preservative efficacy test (PET) — must pass USP 51 criteria
- Skin irritation patch test on 50 volunteers
- Antimicrobial efficacy test — EN 1500 standard (99.9% kill rate confirmed)
- *Quality gate: All 4 tests must pass before batch release*

**Step 6 — Fill, Seal & Dispatch**
- Filled into BPA-free HDPE bottles in ISO Class 7 cleanroom
- Nitrogen-purged headspace to prevent oxidation
- Tamper-evident seal applied
- Batch number, manufacturing date, expiry printed
- *Quality gate: Fill weight check, seal integrity test*

### Final Benefit Claims
- Kills 99.9% of bacteria and viruses (EN 1500 certified)
- Soothes and hydrates — no dry-out effect
- pH 6.5 — matches skin's natural pH
- 100% natural origin actives
- No parabens, no sulphates, no mineral oil

|---|---|

## 2. Lavender & Rose Hand Sanitizer

**Category:** Hand Sanitizer
**Tagline:** Sanitize. Calm. Restore.
**Color Theme:** Purples — #e8e0f4 → #4a3090

### Key Ingredients
| Ingredient | Source | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Lavender Essential Oil | Steam-distilled, Provence, France | Antimicrobial, anxiolytic, calming scent |
| Rose Hydrosol | Bulgarian Damascena rose, steam-distilled | Skin toning, anti-inflammatory, hydration |
| Ethanol 70% | Pharmaceutical-grade | Primary antimicrobial agent |
| Glycerin | Plant-derived | Humectant |
| Linalool | Isolated from lavender | Fragrance stabiliser, antimicrobial booster |
| Geraniol | Rose-derived terpene | Scent fixative, mild antiseptic |

### Process Flow (6 Steps)

**Step 1 — Seasonal Harvest**
- Lavender harvested in July at peak bloom (highest linalool content ~40%)
- Rose petals hand-picked at dawn before heat degrades volatile compounds
- Lavender from Valensole plateau, Provence — AOC certified origin
- Bulgarian Damascena rose from Kazanlak Valley — Rosa Damascena variety only
- *Quality gate: GC-MS terpene profile — linalool >38%, geraniol >18%*

**Step 2 — Steam Distillation**
- Lavender: steam distillation at 100°C, 1 bar pressure, 90-minute cycle
- Rose: hydro-distillation at lower temperature (85°C) to preserve delicate phenols
- Condensate separated — essential oil floats, hydrosol collected separately
- Rose hydrosol retained (not discarded) — contains phenylethanol, citronellol
- Yield: ~1.5kg lavender oil per 100kg flowers; ~1g rose oil per 3kg petals
- *Quality gate: Refractive index, optical rotation, GC-MS purity*

**Step 3 — Ethanol Base Preparation**
- Ethanol 70% prepared and pH-adjusted to 6.0–6.5
- Rose hydrosol replaces 20% of purified water in base — adds toning actives
- *Quality gate: Ethanol concentration verified by densitometry*

**Step 4 — Blending & Emulsification**
- Carbomer gel base prepared
- Lavender essential oil solubilised with polysorbate 20 (natural emulsifier)
- Rose hydrosol blended into gel at 15% concentration
- Linalool and geraniol added at controlled levels (below IFRA limits)
- Glycerin incorporated for moisture retention
- *Quality gate: Clarity test, fragrance stability at 40°C*

**Step 5 — Dermatological Testing**
- HRIPT (Human Repeat Insult Patch Test) — 200 subjects, 9 weeks
- Sensitisation rate: 0% (confirmed hypoallergenic)
- Aromatherapy efficacy study — cortisol reduction measured post-application
- Antimicrobial efficacy: EN 1500 — 99.9% kill rate
- *Quality gate: HRIPT pass, EN 1500 pass*

**Step 6 — Packaging**
- Violet-tinted HDPE bottle (UV protection for photosensitive lavender oil)
- Airless pump dispenser — prevents oxidation of essential oils
- *Quality gate: Pump actuation test, UV transmission test*

### Final Benefit Claims
- Kills 99.9% germs (EN 1500)
- Calming lavender aroma — clinically shown to reduce stress markers
- Rose hydrosol tones and balances skin pH
- 0% parabens, 0% synthetic fragrance
- Hypoallergenic — HRIPT tested

| Glycerin | Plant-derived | Humectant |
| Limonene | Citrus-derived terpene | Fragrance, mild antiseptic |

**Step 1 — Cold-Press Extraction**
- Lemon and orange peels sourced from Nagpur (India's citrus belt)
- Peels cold-pressed within 24 hours of juicing to capture volatile oils
- Bioflavonoid extraction: peels macerated in food-grade ethanol at 25°C for 72 hours
- Limonene separated by centrifugation (used as natural fragrance component)
- *Quality gate: Bioflavonoid concentration >200mg/100g, limonene purity >95%*

**Step 2 — Vitamin E Enrichment**
- Sunflower seeds cold-pressed to yield tocopherol-rich oil
- Vitamin E (d-alpha-tocopherol) isolated via molecular distillation
- Purity: >96% d-alpha-tocopherol (natural form — 2× more bioavailable than synthetic dl-form)
- Ascorbyl glucoside synthesised via enzymatic glycosylation (stable at pH 5–7)
- *Quality gate: HPLC tocopherol assay, stability test at 40°C/6 weeks*

**Step 3 — Ethanol Base**
- Ethanol 70% prepared, pH adjusted to 5.5–6.0 (optimal for Vitamin C stability)
- Lower pH than other variants — preserves ascorbyl glucoside activity
- *Quality gate: pH verification, ascorbyl glucoside stability confirmed*

**Step 4 — Antioxidant Blending**
- Carbomer gel base prepared
- Citrus bioflavonoid extract incorporated at 3% concentration
- Vitamin E solubilised in polysorbate 80, added at 0.5%
- Ascorbyl glucoside added at 1% (brightening dose)
- Limonene added at 0.1% (IFRA compliant) for fresh citrus scent
- Glycerin at 2% for moisture
- *Quality gate: Antioxidant activity test (DPPH assay), colour stability*

**Step 5 — Efficacy Testing**
- Antimicrobial: EN 1500 — 99.9% kill rate confirmed

- Vitamin E skin penetration study: transdermal absorption confirmed at 0.3mm depth
- SPF contribution: bioflavonoids provide SPF 4 (supplementary UV protection)
- *Quality gate: EN 1500 pass, brightening efficacy documented*

**Step 6 — Packaging**
- Amber HDPE bottle (protects Vitamin C and E from UV degradation)
- Disc-top cap for controlled dispensing
- *Quality gate: Light transmission test, fill accuracy ±2%*

### Final Benefit Claims

- Vitamin E repairs and protects skin barrier
- Citrus bioflavonoids brighten skin tone over time
- Ascorbyl glucoside supports collagen synthesis
- 0% sulphates, 0% mineral oil

## 4. Shea & Raw Honey Hand Cream

**Category:** Moisturising Hand Cream
**Tagline:** Repair overnight. Soft by morning.
**Color Theme:** Warm ambers — #f8ede0 → #704820

### Key Ingredients
| Ingredient | Source | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Shea Butter (Unrefined) | West Africa (Ghana/Burkina Faso), fair-trade | Emollient, skin barrier repair, vitamins A+E |
| Raw Wildflower Honey | Cold-processed, Himalayan foothills | Humectant, antibacterial, enzyme-rich |
| Aloe Vera Gel | Organic, cold-pressed | Soothing, anti-inflammatory, hydration |
| Vitamin A (Retinyl Palmitate) | Synthetic, cosmetic grade | Cell turnover, anti-ageing |
| Vitamin E (Tocopherol) | Sunflower-derived | Antioxidant, preservative booster |
| Beeswax | Unbleached, natural | Occlusive layer, texture |
| Cetearyl Alcohol | Plant-derived (coconut) | Emulsifier, cream texture |

### Process Flow (7 Steps)

**Step 1 — Shea Sourcing & Cold-Press**
- Shea nuts hand-collected by women's cooperatives in Ghana (fair-trade certified)
- Nuts sun-dried for 3 days, then roasted lightly to develop flavour compounds
- Cold-pressed in stone mills — unrefined butter retains natural colour (ivory/yellow) and full nutrient profile
- Unrefined shea contains: 5–10% unsaponifiables (triterpenes, tocopherols, phytosterols)
- *Quality gate: Unsaponifiable content >7%, free fatty acid <3%, peroxide value <10*

**Step 2 — Honey Processing**
- Raw wildflower honey sourced from Uttarakhand apiaries (altitude >1500m)
- Cold-processed — never heated above 40°C (preserves enzymes: diastase, invertase, glucose oxidase)
- Filtered through 200-micron mesh (removes wax, pollen debris — retains beneficial pollen)
- Glucose oxidase activity retained — produces H₂O₂ in situ (natural antibacterial)
- *Quality gate: Diastase activity >8 Schade units, HMF <40mg/kg, moisture <20%*

**Step 3 — Aloe Preparation**
- Aloe vera inner gel cold-pressed, filtered to 0.2 micron
- Acemannan polysaccharide concentration verified (>1000mg/L)
- *Quality gate: Acemannan assay, microbial count <100 CFU/g*

**Step 4 — Oil Phase Preparation**
- Shea butter melted at 65°C (minimum melt point)
- Beeswax melted and blended with shea at 70°C
- Cetearyl alcohol added as emulsifier
- Vitamin A (retinyl palmitate) and Vitamin E dissolved in oil phase
- *Quality gate: Oil phase clarity, vitamin assay*

**Step 5 — Water Phase & Emulsification**
- Aloe vera gel + purified water heated to 70°C
- Raw honey dissolved in water phase at 40°C (added after cooling to preserve enzymes)
- Oil phase and water phase combined at 70°C under high-shear mixing (3000 RPM)
- Emulsion cooled to 40°C under slow mixing
- Honey added at 40°C to preserve enzyme activity
- *Quality gate: Emulsion stability test (centrifuge 3000 RPM/30 min — no separation)*

**Step 6 — Stability & Preservation Testing**
- Preservative system: phenoxyethanol 0.8% + ethylhexylglycerin 0.2% (paraben-free)
- PET (Preservative Efficacy Test) — USP 51 Category 2 pass
- 12-week stability at 40°C/75% RH — no phase separation, no colour change
- Skin compatibility: TEWL (transepidermal water loss) reduction measured — 34% reduction after 8 hours
- *Quality gate: PET pass, TEWL study documented*

**Step 7 — Fill & Quality Release**
- Filled into airless pump tubes (prevents honey oxidation)
- Nitrogen-purged to extend shelf life
- Batch release only after QC sign-off on all 6 prior tests
- *Quality gate: Viscosity 15,000–25,000 cP, pH 5.0–5.5, fill weight ±1%*

### Final Benefit Claims
- Repairs dry, cracked skin overnight
- 72-hour moisture lock (occlusive shea + humectant honey)
- Rich in vitamins A & E — supports cell renewal
- Natural antibacterial from honey's glucose oxidase
- 0% mineral oil, 0% parabens, 0% silicones
- Fair-trade shea — ethically sourced

## 5. Collagen & Hyaluronic Acid Hand Cream

**Category:** Moisturising Hand Cream (Anti-Ageing)
**Tagline:** Plump. Firm. Defy time.
**Color Theme:** Aqua blues — #dff0f4 → #1a5060

### Key Ingredients
| Ingredient | Source | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Hydrolysed Marine Collagen Peptides | Deep-sea fish (MSC certified), enzymatic hydrolysis | Skin firming, elasticity, anti-ageing |
| Hyaluronic Acid (High MW) | Microbial fermentation (Streptococcus equi) | Surface hydration, plumping |
| Hyaluronic Acid (Low MW) | Enzymatic degradation of high MW HA | Deep dermal penetration, long-term hydration |
| Vitamin C (Ascorbyl Glucoside) | Enzymatic synthesis | Collagen synthesis stimulation, brightening |
| Ceramide NP | Plant-derived (wheat) | Skin barrier restoration, lipid replenishment |
| Niacinamide (Vitamin B3) | Synthetic, cosmetic grade | Pore minimising, brightening, barrier support |
| Peptide Complex (Matrixyl 3000) | Palmitoyl tripeptide-1 + palmitoyl tetrapeptide-7 | Collagen stimulation, wrinkle reduction |

### Process Flow (7 Steps)

**Step 1 — Marine Collagen Extraction**
- Deep-sea fish skin and scales sourced from MSC-certified sustainable fisheries
- Cleaned, defatted, and demineralised
- Enzymatic hydrolysis using protease enzymes at 55°C for 6 hours
- Hydrolysate filtered through ultrafiltration membrane (MW cut-off: 3000 Da)
- Resulting peptides: average MW 500–2000 Da (optimal for skin absorption)
- Spray-dried to powder form
- *Quality gate: MW distribution by SEC-HPLC, hydroxyproline content >12%, heavy metal screen*

**Step 2 — Dual-Weight Hyaluronic Acid Preparation**
- High MW HA (1.5–1.8 MDa): microbial fermentation, purified by precipitation
- Low MW HA (50–150 kDa): enzymatic degradation of high MW HA using hyaluronidase
- Both grades tested for purity and MW distribution
- High MW: forms surface film — immediate plumping
- Low MW: penetrates stratum corneum — long-term deep hydration
- *Quality gate: MW by viscometry, endotoxin test <0.5 EU/mg*

**Step 3 — Active Ingredient Preparation**
- Ascorbyl glucoside: enzymatic synthesis, stabilised at pH 5.5
- Ceramide NP: extracted from wheat germ, purified by chromatography
- Niacinamide: dissolved in water phase at 5% concentration
- Matrixyl 3000 peptide complex: dissolved in water at 3% (active dose)
- *Quality gate: HPLC purity for each active, stability at pH 5.5*

**Step 4 — Emulsion Base**
- Oil phase: cetearyl alcohol, caprylic/capric triglyceride, squalane (plant-derived)
- Water phase: purified water + niacinamide + ascorbyl glucoside
- High-shear emulsification at 75°C, cooled to 40°C
- *Quality gate: Emulsion droplet size <5 micron (laser diffraction)*

**Step 5 — Active Incorporation**
- Collagen peptides added at 40°C (heat-sensitive)
- Dual-weight HA blend added (0.5% high MW + 0.3% low MW)
- Ceramide NP incorporated via pre-solubilisation in caprylic triglyceride
- Matrixyl 3000 added last (most heat-sensitive)
- pH adjusted to 5.5 (optimal for all actives)
- *Quality gate: Active concentration verified by HPLC, pH 5.3–5.7*

**Step 6 — Clinical Efficacy Testing**
- 8-week clinical study, 60 subjects (age 35–60)
- Skin firmness: Cutometer measurement — 18% improvement
- Wrinkle depth: PRIMOS profilometry — 12% reduction
- Hydration: Corneometer — 45% increase at 24 hours
- Skin barrier: TEWL reduction 28%
- *Quality gate: Clinical data reviewed by dermatologist panel*

**Step 7 — Packaging**
- Airless pump bottle (prevents oxidation of Vitamin C and peptides)
- Dark-tinted bottle (UV protection)
- Nitrogen-purged
- *Quality gate: Pump accuracy ±5%, UV transmission <1%*

### Final Benefit Claims
- Firms and plumps skin — 18% firmness improvement in 8 weeks
- Deep hydration — dual-weight HA penetrates surface and dermis
- Brightens skin tone — Vitamin C + niacinamide
- Restores skin barrier — ceramides replenish lipid layer
- 0% fragrance, 0% parabens, 0% mineral oil
- Clinically tested — dermatologist approved

## 6. Rose & Argan Oil Hand Cream

**Category:** Moisturising Hand Cream (Luxury Nourishing)
**Tagline:** Intensely nourish. Beautifully protect.
**Color Theme:** Rose pinks — #ffe8ec → #903040

### Key Ingredients
| Ingredient | Source | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Rose Extract (CO₂) | Bulgarian Damascena rose, supercritical CO₂ | Anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, skin toning |
| Argan Oil (Cold-Pressed) | Moroccan argan kernels, women's cooperative | Emollient, oleic/linoleic acids, Vitamin E |
| Jojoba Oil (Cold-Pressed) | Desert-grown Simmondsia chinensis | Wax ester — mimics skin sebum, balancing |
| Rosehip Seed Oil | Chilean Rosa canina, cold-pressed | Trans-retinoic acid precursor, brightening |
| Vitamin E (Tocopherol) | Sunflower-derived | Antioxidant, preservative booster |
| Rose Water (Hydrosol) | Steam-distilled Bulgarian rose | Toning, pH balancing, scent |
| Beta-Glucan | Oat-derived | Soothing, immune modulation, wound healing |

### Process Flow (7 Steps)

**Step 1 — CO₂ Rose Extraction**
- Bulgarian Damascena rose petals harvested at dawn in May (peak bloom)
- Supercritical CO₂ extraction at 35°C, 300 bar pressure
- CO₂ acts as solvent — extracts full spectrum of bioactives without heat damage
- Yields: rose wax, phenylethanol, citronellol, geraniol, nerol — all preserved
- Conventional steam distillation would destroy 60% of these compounds
- CO₂ extract is 10× more concentrated than steam-distilled rose oil
- *Quality gate: GC-MS full terpene profile, phenylethanol >60%*

**Step 2 — Argan Oil Cold-Press**
- Argan nuts sourced from Souss-Massa region, Morocco
- Hand-cracked by women's cooperative (Tighanimine) — fair-trade certified
- Cold-pressed in stone mills at <40°C — preserves tocopherols and sterols
- Unfiltered first-press retained (highest polyphenol content)
- Argan oil composition: oleic acid 43%, linoleic acid 36%, Vitamin E 620mg/kg
- *Quality gate: Peroxide value <5 meq/kg, free fatty acid <0.8%, tocopherol assay*

**Step 3 — Supporting Oil Preparation**
- Jojoba: cold-pressed, golden unrefined — wax ester structure identical to human sebum
- Rosehip: cold-pressed Chilean rosehip seeds — trans-retinoic acid precursor (tretinoin-like effect)
- Both oils nitrogen-flushed to prevent rancidity
- *Quality gate: Iodine value, saponification value, oxidation stability (Rancimat test)*

**Step 4 — Rose Water Preparation**
- Steam-distilled Bulgarian rose hydrosol (by-product of rose oil distillation)
- pH 4.8–5.2 (naturally acidic — matches skin pH)
- Phenylethanol content: 0.1–0.3% (natural preservative contribution)
- *Quality gate: pH, microbial count <10 CFU/mL, phenylethanol by GC*

**Step 5 — Emulsion Formulation**
- Oil phase: argan + jojoba + rosehip + CO₂ rose extract + Vitamin E
- Water phase: rose water (replacing 40% of purified water) + beta-glucan
- Emulsification at 70°C with cetearyl glucoside (natural emulsifier from coconut+glucose)
- Cooled to 40°C — CO₂ rose extract added last (most volatile)
- pH adjusted to 5.0–5.5 with citric acid (natural)
- *Quality gate: Emulsion stability, droplet size <3 micron, pH 5.0–5.5*

**Step 6 — Sensory & Efficacy Testing**
- Skin softness: Frictiometer measurement — 31% improvement after 1 application
- Moisture retention: Corneometer — 52% increase at 8 hours
- Anti-inflammatory: IL-6 cytokine reduction assay — 22% reduction
- Sensory panel: 95% rated texture as "luxurious" or "silky"
- Rancidity stability: Rancimat test — 24-month shelf life confirmed
- *Quality gate: All sensory and efficacy benchmarks met*

**Step 7 — Luxury Packaging**
- Frosted glass jar with rose-gold aluminium lid (premium positioning)
- Inner seal: food-grade aluminium foil (prevents oxidation)
- Outer box: FSC-certified recycled card
- *Quality gate: Seal integrity, jar drop test, packaging compatibility*

### Final Benefit Claims
- Intensely nourishes very dry, damaged hands
- Anti-inflammatory rose — reduces redness and irritation
- Argan oil balances skin — suitable for all skin types
- Jojoba mimics sebum — non-greasy, fast-absorbing
- Rosehip brightens and supports cell renewal
- 0% silicones, 0% mineral oil, 0% synthetic fragrance
- Fair-trade argan — ethically sourced from Morocco
