## 1. Hand Sanitizer Series
**Base Formula:** 70% v/v Pharmaceutical Grade Ethanol ($C_2H_5OH$)

### 1.1 Aloe & Green Tea Hand Sanitizer
*   **Ethanol ($C_2H_5OH$):** 70% v/v (Primary Antimicrobial)
*   **Aloe Vera Gel ($ (C_6H_{10}O_5)_n $):** 8.0% (Humectant/Soothe)
*   **Vegetable Glycerin ($C_3H_8O_3$):** 2.0% (Moisture lock)
*   **Green Tea Extract (EGCG - $C_{22}H_{18}O_{11}$):** 1.5% (Antioxidant boost)
*   **Carbomer:** 0.5% (Gel thickener)
*   **Fragrance:** 0.3% (Natural terpene blend)

### 1.2 Lavender & Rose Hand Sanitizer
*   **Ethanol ($C_2H_5OH$):** 70% v/v
*   **Rose Hydrosol ($C_8H_{10}O$ - Phenylethanol):** 15.0% (Toning agent)
*   **Lavender Essential Oil ($C_{10}H_{18}O$ - Linalool):** 1.0% (Calming active)
*   **Glycerin ($C_3H_8O_3$):** 2.0%
*   **Linalool & Geraniol:** <0.5% (Terpene isolates for stability)

### 1.3 Citrus & Vitamin E Hand Sanitizer
*   **Ethanol ($C_2H_5OH$):** 70% v/v
*   **Citrus Bioflavonoid Extract:** 3.0% (Brightening agent)
*   **Ascorbyl Glucoside ($C_{12}H_{18}O_{11}$ - Vit C):** 1.0% (Collagen support)
*   **Vitamin E (Tocopherol - $C_{29}H_{50}O_2$):** 0.5% (Skin repair)
*   **Glycerin ($C_3H_8O_3$):** 2.0%
*   **Limonene ($C_{10}H_{16}$):** 0.1% (Natural scent)
